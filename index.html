Process

How it works

From first contact to final deed — one continuous, verified flow.

01

Verified profile

Buyers verify their identity via iDIN and connect financial data via Ockto. One profile, valid across every Nexum transaction.

02

Transparent bidding

Every bid is logged immutably. Buyers see the full bid log in real time. No hidden bids, no information asymmetry.

03

Mortgage auction

Banks compete live for the buyer's mortgage. Time-to-Yes under 5 minutes. Financing certainty before the sale is final.

04

Digital deed

The notary receives a pre-filled dossier. AI populates the deed template. Blockchain anchors the final transfer. Done.
